Sandwich Recipe

Description

A sandwich is a versatile, portable meal consisting of fillings like meat, cheese, vegetables, and spreads, layered between two or more slices of bread or within a split roll.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of white/brown bread
  • Butter
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 2 slices of ham/turkey/chicken
  • Lettuce, tomato, cucumber (optional)
  • Mustard/mayonnaise (optional)

Steps

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Layer the ingredients on one slice of bread in the following order: cheese, meat, vegetables (if using).
  3. Top with the second slice of bread, buttered side facing down.
  4. Cut the sandwich in half diagonally or straight down the middle.
